Campus Life

Music room

Was established in 2014

Music is a vital part of education here at Velammal Vidhyashram and we ensure that students get an opportunity to play and enjoy all forms of music. Velammal Vidhyashram has two Music Rooms that house instruments that students can play during music classes or club classes. The Music room is used as a place for students to practice and play their preferred instruments. Students who choose to be part of the Music Club may also bring their own instruments. Our instruments include a complete drum kit, keyboards, and guitars (both acoustic and electric), among others. Music is integrated into the curriculum from the kindergarten years so that children develop a love for it. In the early years, children are made aware of rhythm and melody through group singing of songs and rhymes. Our music rooms fitted with state-of-the-art instruments and run by professionally-trained and qualified music teachers, provide the perfect environment for children to learn and appreciate music. Children in the younger grades have Western Music classes incorporated into their daily schedules, allowing them to unwind and express themselves in the most creative way possible! Students in Middle and High School get the opportunity to continue their musical training, if they choose, by becoming part of the Velammal Vidhyashram Music Club. The Music Club classes include training in vocals and instruments. Our fully-equipped music rooms allow students to explore and learn to lay western instruments such as drums, guitar, keyboard, etc. as well as Indian instruments such as tabla, dholak, daphli, etc. The classes are designed to facilitate learning for all students who become members at their level.

School Achievments

Inter School Quiz Competition
Won Second Prize on Inter School Quiz Competition

The Inter-School Quiz Competition brings together students from different schools to showcase their knowledge and critical thinking skills. It involves various rounds of challenging questions across different subjects. The competition promotes healthy competition, learning, and teamwork. It offers a platform for students to demonstrate their intellectual prowess and build connections with peers. The event culminates in a final round where the highest-scoring team or individual is declared the winner. The competition enhances students' knowledge, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities while fostering camaraderie and personal growth.


Inter School Writting Competition
Won First Prize on Inter School Writting Competition

The Inter-School Writing Competition is a platform for students to showcase their writing skills and creativity. It includes various categories like short stories, poetry, and essays. Participants submit their work based on specific themes or prompts, which are evaluated by judges. The competition promotes literary exchange and offers valuable learning experiences for students to refine their writing abilities. It fosters a sense of community and provides recognition for young writers.


Inter School Recitation Competition
Won Second Prize on Inter School Recitation Competition

The Inter-School Recitation Competition is a platform where students showcase their skills in reciting poetry or literary pieces. It encourages students to exhibit their expressive abilities, memorization skills, and interpretation of written works. The competition promotes appreciation for literature and offers an opportunity for participants to shine. It enhances students' public speaking skills, boosts confidence, and fosters a love for the spoken word.

What's new

  • School Canteen

    The school canteen is an integral part of any educational institution, providing a space for students to enjoy their meals and socialize with their peers. It serves as a hub of activity during break times, offering a variety of food and beverage options to cater to the diverse tastes and preferences of students. One of the primary functions of a school canteen is to provide nutritious meals that contribute to the overall well-being of students. Canteens often offer a balanced menu that includes a combination of carbohydrates, proteins, fruits, vegetables, and dairy products. This ensures that students have access to wholesome meals that help sustain their energy levels throughout the day and support their physical and mental development. In conclusion, the school canteen plays a vital role in providing students with nutritious meals, promoting healthy eating habits, fostering social interactions, and developing life skills. By prioritizing the well-being of students and maintaining high standards of food quality and safety, the school canteen can contribute significantly to the overall educational experience and the health of the student body.

  • Attendance

    Attendance refers to the act of being present or actively participating in a particular event, activity, or institution. In the context of education, attendance is a crucial aspect that directly impacts a student's academic progress and overall performance. Regular attendance in school is essential for several reasons. Firstly, consistent attendance ensures that students do not miss out on valuable classroom instruction and academic content. Attending classes regularly allows students to engage with the curriculum, participate in discussions, ask questions, and receive guidance from teachers. This active involvement promotes a deeper understanding of the subjects being taught and enhances the learning experience. In conclusion Regular attendance in school is crucial for academic progress and personal development. It ensures students don't miss out on learning, fosters discipline, and promotes peer interaction. Attendance tracking helps identify issues and provide necessary support. Open communication between schools and parents is key.
